The current chairman and vice-chairman of the Chamber of Commerce and Industry are Mr Jayant Padarath and Mr Paul de Baas respectively. The secretary is Mrs. Joanne Pancham. The day-to-day management of the House rests with the chairman and the secretary.

The Board

The board is formed by representatives of the branches. Each business group is represented on the board by one member. The 29 members elect a board from among their number every 2 years.

The current board consists of the following MPs:
