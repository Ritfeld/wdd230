The Benefit of Joining The Chamber

What does Chamber of Commerce stand for?

Chamber of Commerce and Industry stands for to promote opportunities in entrepreneurship and thereby promote economic growth in Suriname.

What services offer Chamber of Commerce & Industry to citizens?

You can contact the KKF for all information regarding the Trade Register, stock exchanges, Suriname Directory, the 'One Stop Window', and Entrepreneurship Support Center (ESC).

The activities that the KKF undertakes include organizing business evenings. Organizing discussion evenings. Giving presentations, training and education. Organizing fairs.

What is the biggest event organized by Chamber of Commerce & Industry?

The Jaarbeurs is organized annually from the end of November to the beginning of December. All sectors participate in this successful business fair, both locally and internationally. This is the largest annual general fair in the Caribbean, attracting 100,000 visitors.

Another important fair held by the KKF is the De AGRO - ICT - MADE IN SURINAME FAIR organized annually from late April to early May. Participants are: Agriculture, horticulture and forestry, as well as flower, plant, fruit, livestock and beekeeping. Service (financial, ICT, transport, real estate, research, information, training, etc.), supply and processing companies. Industry (food and beverage, building materials, furniture, chemical, metal, clothing and packaging), mining, tourism, fishing and fish processing, construction, art, home crafts and industry. Dealers' of production resources (machinery, rolling stock, computers, factory installations, equipment, instruments, etc.).
